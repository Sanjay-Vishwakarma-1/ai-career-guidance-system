AI Decision Support Workflow

Our AI-assisted Career Guidance System analyzes student skills, compares them with career-specific requirements from the career dataset, identifies missing skills, calculates career readiness, and generates a personalized learning roadmap to support students in making informed career decisions.

1
Student Input Collection

The system collects student information including name, academic year, career interest, and existing skills.

Name Academic Year Career Interest Skills (comma separated)
↓
2
Text Cleaning & Skill Normalization

The system processes the student's skill input by removing extra spaces, converting to lowercase, and splitting by commas to normalize the data.

"Python, SQL, Excel" → ['python', 'sql', 'excel']
↓
3
Career Dataset Lookup

The system retrieves required skills for the selected career from the structured career dataset.

Career Database (20+ careers) Dataset Lookup
Career: Data Analyst → Required Skills:
✅ Python, SQL, Excel
❌ Pandas, Power BI, Tableau, Statistics
↓
4
Skill Gap Analysis

The system compares the student's normalized skills with the required skills from the selected career to identify missing competencies and learning priorities.

✅ Learned: 4 skills ❌ Missing: 4 skills
↓
5
Career Readiness Score Calculation

The system calculates the match score using the formula:

4 Learned Skills ÷ 8 Required Skills × 100 = 50% Readiness
↓
6
Decision Support Output

The system generates a personalized learning pathway and explains:

✔ Why this career was recommended ✔ Which skills are missing ✔ Which skills should be learned first ✔ Which roadmap should be followed

Responsible AI Considerations

Fairness

The system recommends all 20+ careers without bias. Recommendations are based solely on skills matching, not on gender, background, or any personal attributes.

Transparency

The system clearly explains how recommendations are generated, including the skill comparison logic and the formula used to calculate the readiness score.

Ethics

The system promotes sustainable careers and does not provide harmful, discriminatory, or misleading recommendations.

Privacy

No personal data is stored permanently. All student information is processed ephemerally and is not shared with third parties.

Our AI system is designed with responsible AI principles to ensure fairness, transparency, ethics, and privacy for all users.
