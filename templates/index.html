AI Decision Support Workflow

Our AI-assisted Career Guidance System analyzes student skills, compares them with career-specific requirements stored in the career dataset, identifies missing skills, calculates career readiness, and generates a personalized learning roadmap to support students in making informed career decisions.

1
Student Input Collection

The system collects student information including name, academic year, career interest, and existing skills.

Name Academic Year Career Interest Skills (comma separated)
↓
2
Text Cleaning & Skill Normalization

The system processes the student's skill input by removing extra spaces, converting to lowercase, and splitting by commas to normalize the data.

"Python, SQL, Excel" → ['python', 'sql', 'excel']
↓
3
Career Dataset Retrieval

The decision-support engine retrieves the required skills for the selected career from the career dataset.

Career Database (20+ careers) Required Skills Mapping
Career: Data Analyst → Required Skills:
✅ Python, SQL, Excel
❌ Pandas, Power BI, Tableau, Statistics
↓
4
Skill Comparison & Gap Detection

The decision-support engine compares the student's normalized skills with the required skills from the selected career to identify missing competencies and learning priorities.

✅ Learned: 4 skills ❌ Missing: 4 skills
↓
5
Career Readiness Score Calculation

The system calculates the match score using the formula:

4 Learned Skills ÷ 8 Required Skills × 100 = 50% Readiness
↓
6
Decision Support Output

The system generates a personalized learning pathway and explains:

✔ Why this career was recommended ✔ Which skills are missing ✔ Which skills should be learned first ✔ Which roadmap should be followed
